The Perfect Scrambled Egg

Description

Silky, smooth, delicious. This easy scrambled egg method will up your breakfast game and impress even the likes of Gordon Ramsay. Using top quality eggs, a sieve and your best non-stick pan, you are certain to produce scrambled eggs like you never have before. Let's go!

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tbsp butter
  • Optional: creme fraiche or greek yogurt
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Whisk eggs, salt and pepper in mixing bowl. Hold bowl at an angle, whisking in a circular motion to maximize silkiness potential.
  2. Set burner to medium heat and preheat your best non-stick skillet.
  3. Using a sieve, pour eggs into skillet and begin whisking them constantly.
  4. Cook until they reach a homogeneous thickness, setting with a small curd. Optionally, stir in creme fraiche or greek yogurt to reach an even smoother texture.
  5. Plate and add more salt and pepper to taste. Delicious on a piece of homemade sourdough bread. Enjoy!
