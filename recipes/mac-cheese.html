Elevated Annie's Mac & Cheese

Description

Don't get me wrong, Annie's Mac & Cheese is pretty darn good on it's own. What if I told you it could be elevated to taste even better, with only a few additional ingredients and steps? This recipe is versatile, so feel free to use whatever vegetables/spices you may have on hand. Let's dive in!

Ingredients

  • 1 box Annie's Mac and Cheese (I prefer "Shells and White Cheddar")
  • Splash of milk
  • 2-3 tbsp butter
  • 1/2 a small yellow onion, diced
  • 1/2 cup frozen peas
  • 1/2 red pepper, diced
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/2 tsp chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp cumin
  • Salt and pepper for tasting
  • Optional toppings: shredded cheese, bacon, green onion, jalapeno, etc.

Steps

  1. Boil water and cook pasta according to box instructions.
  2. In a large skillet, melt 1 tbsp butter and saute onion and red pepper on medium heat until tender.
  3. Add in peas and spices, cooking until peas are cooked and spices are incorporated.
  4. Add cheese packet, butter, milk, and cooked/drained pasta to skillet. Mix until incorporated.
  5. Season with salt and pepper to taste. Add optional toppings such as extra cheese, bacon bits, or your favorite sauce. Enjoy!
